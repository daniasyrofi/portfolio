About

Hey, I am Dani.

As a product designer, I want to share what I have learned, hoping that the knowledge keeps spreading rather than stopping with me.

Over five years, I have worked across studios, startups, and as a solo freelancer. From Kretya Studio's 3.4 million view Dribbble feed to solo work that helped a computer vision construction startup land pre seed funding. Today I lead product design at Abadikan, shaping both product and brand surfaces for a growing Indonesian company.

My focus is design systems, prototyping, and user research, anchored in Figma and shipped through close collaboration with engineering. I believe good design is about impact, not just visuals. The difference between "Transaction failed" and "We could not process this, your money is safe" is not microcopy. It is empathy in the interface.

When I am not designing, I write on Bootcamp and Muzli, learn new tools, and think about how AI is quietly reshaping the craft. If that sounds interesting, let us talk.
