About

The short version.

I’m a Product Designer based in Jakarta with five years of hands‑on experience across UI / UX and digital product design. My practice is built around Figma design systems, SaaS platforms, and end‑to‑end product delivery.

I’ve shipped 20+ projects across SaaS, Fintech, Healthcare, and Construction Tech, helping startups move from MVP to pre‑seed and beyond. The through line is the same every time. Working close with cross‑functional teams, C‑level stakeholders, and developers to deliver interfaces that are user‑friendly, scalable, and a little quieter than they need to be.

Off‑hours, I write about design on Medium. Essays on training design judgment, AI splitting designers by taste, and why great logo systems don’t come from talent. Studying at BINUS University in Digital Business Management.
